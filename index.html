About The Mixologist

Welcome

One Stop Bar Solutions
Since 2013

From being revered, to being shunned and back to being revered again, bartending has not always been the easiest trade to get mixed-up in. It is this volatile history that has seen bartending evolve and adapt to not only survive, but advance artistically and scientifically to newer heights.

We love bartending. And by that I mean we have pictures of rare Wine bottles plastered on our bedroom walls. And you know what they say; do what you love. So we did.
